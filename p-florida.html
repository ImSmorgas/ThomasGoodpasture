Team Project

Project Overview

As part of a recent school assignment, my six classmates and I collaborated on a team project. With a total of seven people, each team member was tasked with creating their own page for the site. The project had intentionally minimal requirements to allow for creative freedom and a shared vision among the team.

The key requirements included creating mockups of our respective sections using Figma, ensuring representation for desktop, mobile, and tablet views. The ultimate goal was to align the final version of our website closely with the initial mockups. This approach allowed us the flexibility to explore and implement our ideas while maintaining a cohesive and visually appealing end result.

Desired Learning Objectives

01. Collaborate Efficiently in a Team Environment

Given the chance to collaborate within a team of designers, I proactively sought to make a meaningful impact on the project. Recognizing the importance of teamwork, I focused on practicing key factors to enhance our collective effectiveness. This included holding both myself and my team members accountable, maintaining open and effective communication, empowering teammates when needed, and ensuring a shared vision across all sections.

Through these intentional actions during collaborative moments, trust and harmony were fostered within the team. My goal was to not only contribute individually but also to cultivate an environment where everyone felt valued, appreciated, and aligned towards a common goal.
