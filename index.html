About Me

Hello, I'm Thomas Goodpasture, a dedicated Digital Media Design and Production student, with a focus in web design. I am currently in my final semester at Chattanooga State with a GPA of 3.69. Currently, I'm enrolled in the Google-led UX/UI Design Bootcamp, where I'm making great progress in course 6 out of 7.

What truly drives me is the opportunity to create captivating and user-friendly website experiences. I love exploring the possibilities offered by HTML , JS, and CSS, always looking to expand my creative boundaries. I also utilize Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, XD, and other platforms such as Figma to help facilitate my creative flow.

I'm excited about the prospect of bringing my skills and passion to your project, creating a digital experience that stands out.
